## 0.1.0 — 2026-06-27

### Added

- **World reaction** (`Achieve.Tempo.World`): `WorldReaction` spreading fire/water/ice/electricity by
  rules on a flat grid, per-cell `ElementalState`, `Material`, table-driven `ReactionRule`
  (single-field `;` input), and the live-event `ReactionHandle`.
- **Director** (`Achieve.Tempo.Director`): `DirectorBrain` with a default policy extended via `virtual`,
  the `SeedAction` seeding action plus four built-ins (`DryUpwind`/`IgniteUpwind`/`ChargeStorm`/`SummonRain`),
  and `PhaseFsm` phase switching (hysteresis).
- **Tension** (`Achieve.Tempo.Tension`): `TensionModel` combining two signals into 0–1 (rise fast, fall slow).
- **Config** (`Achieve.Tempo.Config`): the code-separated data center `DataService<T>` and
  `TensionConfig`/`PhaseData`/`ActionData`/`RuleData` (with defaults).
- **Editor tools** (`Achieve.Tempo.Editor`): `RuleValidator` (pre-build rule-table check) and
  `DebugWindow` (live tension/phase view).
- **Tests**: EditMode (`TensionModel`/`PhaseFsm`/`ReactionRule`/`RuleValidator`),
  PlayMode (spread direction, quench, event cleanup).
- **Sample**: `Samples~/BasicGrassFire` — the simplest single loop.
- **Docs**: fumadocs site (ko/en/ja/zh).
